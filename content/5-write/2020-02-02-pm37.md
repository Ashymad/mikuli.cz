Here comes,  
the pain,  
but I  
flush it straight down the drain  

Go dis-  
appear,  
clockwise,  
in southern hemisphere  

Too much,  
may clog,  
my mind,  
with tangled lumps of fog  

I want,  
you there,  
hold me,  
your words are my plunger
