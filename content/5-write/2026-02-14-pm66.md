And every day i wake,  
I hope that it's not fake,  
The bed with you and me,  
The pillows, cats, the peace,  
Of early morning bliss.  

It feels like a cocoon,  
And even though so soon,  
You wake up sleepy face,  
The cats scream into space,  
City bursts into dance,  
Some work falls in my hands,  
Yet I can be assured,  
It all can be endured,  
Cause just before all this,  
Inside our cocoon's bliss,  
I hugged you very tight,  
It felt so very right,  
When whisper left my lips:  
"I'm loving you to bits."
