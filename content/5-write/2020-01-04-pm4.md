Pause,  
Now reach,  
Into the heart,  

See?  
Its them,  
they need to leave  

Thoughts,  
feelings,  
my mind is chained  

Look,  
its her,  
she holds the chain  

Yes,  
I know,  
"Just cut the chain"  

Though,  
It hurts,  
It's all I have
