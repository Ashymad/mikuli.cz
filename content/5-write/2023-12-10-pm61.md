Cause I don't wanna sleep,  
If that would make me see,  
Another day alike,  
Come sooner  

The fleeting hours pass,  
Though I wish they could just,  
Slow down and let me think,  
Let me breathe  

Had only my eyes closed,  
All future dreams arose,  
Scared me not to open,  
Them again
