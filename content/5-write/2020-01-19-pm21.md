I turn my head,  
here on my bed,  

look to the side,  
eyes open wide,  

there's noone there,  
but yet, somewhere,  

you turn your head,  
there on your bed,  

look to the side,  
eyes open wide,  

our minds then see,  
in bed are we,  

 In ear,  
 I hear,  
 soft "Morning, dear"
