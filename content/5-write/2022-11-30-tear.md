---
Title: Tear
Published: 2022-11-30
Author: Szymon  
Layout: blog  
Tag: poetry  
---

With every tear,  
I cannot catch,  
your pangs of pain,  
I cannot match,  

WIth every thought,  
strangling your heart,  
hugs that don't reach,  
when we're apart,  

I check a mark,  
hug from the past,  
that by now will,  
forever last.
