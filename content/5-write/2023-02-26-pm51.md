What would you do,  
If your heart,  
Was made,  
Of tinfoil.  

Would you try to,  
Flatten it,  
Each time,  
It's balled up?  

Bring it back to,  
It's old state,  
Shiny,  
Unblemished?  

Or would you let,  
It keep all,  
The spikes,  
Crevices.
