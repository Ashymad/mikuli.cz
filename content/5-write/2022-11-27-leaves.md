---
Title: Leaves
Published: 2022-11-27
Author: Szymon  
Layout: blog  
Tag: poetry  
---

Leaves redden and fall,  
crunching satisfies my feet,  
my heart answers your call,  
dancing signifies it's beat,  

around we spin so fast,  
in clouds of escaping leaves,  
let it forever last,  
let us forget how we missed.
