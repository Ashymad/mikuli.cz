Shy shimmer,  
shards of a shadow,  
fast flow,  
flowing free,  

Harsh hurt,  
Has home in the heart,  
blooming blue,  
beating blood  

Green grass,  
Grows ghost of gloom,  
Stern steps,  
Start strolling away.
